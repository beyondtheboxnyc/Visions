City Steam

City Steam are 4 creative and cost-effective projects to nail down the steam issues in New York City.
Vision One > City Steam > Project V1.1

Current Problem

It's 2019, and New York City still can't figure out a way to make traffic flow as smooth as it should to be. This NYC's 'daily' problem is slowing vehicles whihc creates a sequence of events that blocks crosswalks.

Our Solution

At a glance, the steam flow into a bump.
Our current solution is very simple and very efficient, it outsmarted the current need of renewing NYC's aged infrastructure, to easily install an extended pipe that gets the steam flow into a road bump toward the curb, to allow cars running over without slowing other vehicles and creating a sequence of events that blocks crosswalks.

Potential Partners

Con-Ed is the entity responsible for steam pipes in New York City.  We understand that Con-Ed is a for-profit, investor-owned company and may have no interest in public concerns such as making NYC's traffic better.  We do understand these challenges and we'll work on the funding side, in case we'll get a chance from the City and State Authorities!

City Delivery

City Delivery, reinventing the way trucks deliver freight in mega cities like New York.
Vision One > City Delivery > Project V1.5

Current Problem

In New York City, trucks are double-parked, sometimes triple-parked which creates traffic jams everywhere.

DOT's current strategy is not working:

Since there is no effective invention yet! By law, the city authorities cannot enforce the Off-Hour Deliveries, and the DOT has no choice but encouraging businesses, which is not prioritized by both delivery trucks and local businesses.

Our Solution is an
